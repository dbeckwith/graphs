Help

Click on the display area to create a vertex. You can drag vertices around to rearrange them. Click on a vertex to select it, and it will turn green. Click another vertex to create an edge between them. Hold SHIFT when connecting vertices to keep selecting the vertex you click on. Click on a blank area to deselect a vertex. CTRL+click on a vertex to delete it and any edges connected to it. Connecting two vertices that are already connected will delete that edge.
